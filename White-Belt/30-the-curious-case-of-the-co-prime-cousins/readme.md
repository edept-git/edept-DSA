### Description
Given two positive integers, determine if they are co-prime (i.e., their greatest common divisor (GCD) is 1).

### Constraints
- 1 ≤ a, b ≤ 1000

### Example
Input: 15, 22
Output: Yes

Input: 12, 18
Output: No

### Concepts Covered
- Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)
- Euclidean Algorithm (Optional, but recommended for efficiency)
